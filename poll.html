What is Pollution ?

In today's interconnected world, pollution has emerged as a critical threat to our environment and well-being. The unchecked release of pollutants into the air, water, and soil poses severe risks to ecosystems, wildlife, and human health. From industrial emissions to plastic waste, the global impact of pollution is undeniable, contributing to climate change, biodiversity loss, and public health crises. Urgent collective action is required to address the root causes, implement sustainable practices, and foster a cleaner, more resilient planet for current and future generations.
