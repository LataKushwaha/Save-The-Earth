ENDANGERED SPECIES

Save endangered species, save our planet.

What are endangered species

An endangered species is a type of organism that is threatened by extinction. Species become endangered for two main reasons: loss of habitat and loss of genetic variation. A loss of habitat can happen naturally. Dinosaurs, for instance, lost their habitat about 65 million years ago.


  • Causes that lead to extinction of species

    1. Habitat loss
    2. Population growth
    3. Climate change

  • How to save Endangered species

  • Protect habitats
  • Avoiding pesticides and chemicals which cause damage to the environment
  • Reduse pollution
